What are the must-have items for any equestrian wardrobe?

A lot of people think that owning a horse means you need to get a fancy new wardrobe with lots of fancy new clothes. In reality, a horse riding wardrobe is about comfort, practicality and functionality. You don’t need a lot of fancy stuff to look good while riding, because you spend most of your time on horseback in jeans and a T-shirt (or even nothing at all!). However, there are a few things that you should have in your wardrobe to make sure you’re looking your best and feeling comfortable during both training sessions and trail rides. A horse riding wardrobe consists of both functional and fashionable pieces that you can mix and match to create different looks – just like you would in your everyday wardrobe. Depending on your personal taste and the season you can have a more active or relaxed look. If you want to have an all-purpose wardrobe that covers you for all seasons and all types of riding then these are some of the must-have items you need:- Horse riding boots - These are a must have in any horse riding wardrobe. You can find a wide range of different styles of riding boots from dress to casual. You need to find a pair of boots that are the right size for you and that fit well. If you have a wide or short leg then you will want to go for a slightly wider boot than if you have a thinner leg. Make sure that your boots have good support and are well padded so they don’t put too much pressure on your horse’s hooves.
