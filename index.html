The best equestrian clothing and where to find them

What are the best brands for equestrian clothing?

Equestrian clothing is designed to be both functional and stylish. It’s not just about staying warm or dry – it’s also about looking good and feeling confident while Horse Riding. This makes choosing the right clothing for your sport important. Fortunately, there are many excellent brands of equestrian clothing available today. The most important thing to keep in mind when buying equestrian clothing is finding a brand that suits your personal style and budget. There are many different brands that produce quality equestrian clothing at various price points and styles. The best brands will depend on your personal preferences and the types of clothing you’ll need for specific activities. Whether you’re a casual rider, taking horse riding lessons, or an avid competitor, there’s sure to be something for you among these top equestrian clothing brands.

Basics Of Equestrian Clothing


When it comes to choosing the best equestrian clothing brands, one of the first things you’ll want to look at is the fabric and style of the clothing. There are three main fabrics used for equestrian clothing: woven, woven/spandex blend, and knit. Woven fabric is the most common type of fabric used for many equestrian clothing items. Woven fabric is made by interlocking threads to create a fabric that has both strength and stretch. Woven fabrics tend to be strong, durable, and long-lasting. They also have excellent wrinkle resistance and are often very breathable, which is important for many types of equestrian clothing. Spandex blend fabrics are made up of some combination of spandex and other synthetic fibers. These fabrics are often more stretchy than traditional spandex fabrics, making them ideal for many types of equestrian clothing. Knee-length knit fabrics are also popular in equestrian clothing. Knit fabrics are made by interlocking threads on a machine in a criss-cross pattern. This process creates a fabric that is extremely durable, yet soft and flexible. These fabrics are often very breathable, making them ideal for warm weather and certain outdoor activities such as Eventing or Dressage.

Dressage Brands

Dressage clothing is made to be both functional and stylish. Many brands produce dressage clothing with a focus on function and quality. Some dressage clothing brands also produce outdoor clothing. This makes choosing the best dressage clothing brands for you a little more difficult. However, there are some dressage clothing brands that stand above the rest. Kensington is one of the most popular dressage clothing brands. This brand produces a wide range of excellent dressage clothing at affordable prices. Another excellent brand is Tredstep. This brand produces high-quality dressage clothing that is very similar to Kensington. Another great option is Swiss Trail. This brand produces excellent quality dressage clothing at an affordable price. Swiss Trail is one of the few brands that produce both indoor and outdoor clothing.

Casual Clothing For Equestrians

Casual equestrian clothing like SaddleBar is designed for the less formal rider. These types of clothing are usually made of woven fabrics with spandex blend fabrics and some knit fabrics used in the construction of certain items. Casual equestrian clothing is great for casual events, trail rides and casual trail runs. Casual equestrian clothing is often more affordable than other types of equestrian clothing. This makes it a great option for those who need to outfit a large group of riders.

Competition Clothing

Competition clothing is designed to be both functional and fashionable. These types of clothing are often made with the same fabrics and construction techniques as casual equestrian clothing. Competition clothing is often more expensive than casual equestrian clothing. However, it is also often made with higher-quality materials that are designed to stand up to the rigors of competition riding. Competition clothing is often made with wool or polyester mixes. A few popular brands of competitive clothing are Ariat, Trek, and Smartwool. These brands produce excellent quality competition clothing that is also very fashionable. Ariat and Trek are two of the most popular brands of competition clothing.

Hybrid Brands

Hybrid brands produce clothing that is great for both casual and formal events. This type of clothing is often made with the same fabrics and construction techniques as casual equestrian clothing. Hybrid brands often produce competitive clothing that can be worn casually. This makes them a great choice for those who need to outfit a large group of riders.

Conclusion

Equestrian clothing is designed to be both functional and fashionable. It’s not just about staying warm or dry – it’s also about looking good and feeling confident while riding.
