What are some myths about equestrian clothing?

You love your horse, but sometimes you wish you could just throw on a pair of jeans and a t-shirt and be done with it. Unfortunately, you can’t. As a proud owner of a horse, you have to make sure you’re well-equipped at all times. You never know when something unexpected might happen when you’re out riding or just around other horse-enthusiasts. There are plenty of myths floating around about equestrian clothing, especially when it comes to jeans. Some people even claim that you can’t ride in jeans at all! Well, get ready to say goodbye to those misconceptions because these are some myths busted on jeans and everything else in the equestrian world. Stay informed and informed with these facts about equestrian clothing.

Jeans are not professional equestrian clothing

Like we just mentioned, there are a few misconceptions floating around about jeans and their place in equestrian clothing. Some people even believe that you can’t ride in jeans at all! Well, that couldn’t be further from the truth. Jeans are actually more suitable for outdoor riding than any other type of clothing. Since jeans are made of a heavier fabric and have a higher waterproof level, they are ideal for riding in wet or muddy conditions. Plus, they have plenty of pockets for storing essential riding tools and items.

You can’t ride in jeans and a top at the same time

While it is possible to ride in jeans and a top at the same time, it is not recommended. First of all, you will probably look like a fashion disaster. Secondly, you won’t be able to adjust your top to stay dry in the rain. If you really want to wear both your jeans and a top at the same time, make sure to take them off as soon as you get to your ride. Wear a pair of rain pants over your jeans to stay dry and put on a nice top that will stay in place even when you’re riding.

You can’t ride in shorts or capri pants

Another misconception that has been busted is that you can’t ride in shorts or capri pants. This is actually not true either. You can ride in either of these types of pants as long as they are a pair of breakaway pants. Breakaway pants are made with a special material that breaks away easily from a rider’s legs if they fall off the horse or are caught in the stirrups. If you want to keep the look simple and classic, breakaway pants can be your best option for riding in shorts.

You have to buy new clothes for every single season

Another misconception that has been busted is that you have to buy new clothes for every single season. In fact, you only need to buy new clothes for the most extreme weather conditions. You don’t have to buy new clothes every time the seasons change unless there is a specific hazard that you need to protect against. While you do need to be prepared for extreme weather conditions such as extreme heat or cold, you don’t need to buy new clothes for every season.

You can’t wear a helmet and a shirt at the same time

Another misconception is that you can’t wear a helmet and a shirt at the same time. This is false. You can wear a helmet and a shirt at the same time as long as you follow a few simple rules. First of all, you should avoid the “shirt over the helmet” look. This is not only unprofessional, but it can also be dangerous. If you’re going to wear a shirt over your helmet, you should make sure it is long enough to cover your pants. Secondly, you should only wear a T-shirt if you don’t need to wear a jacket. Wrapping a T-shirt around your waist isn’t a good idea either.

Conclusion

There are plenty of misconceptions surrounding equestrian clothing. Some people believe that you can’t ride in jeans and others think you can only wear a helmet in one of two different styles. Fortunately, these and many other misconceptions have been busted. Now that you know the truth about equestrian clothing, you can be confident that you are wearing the right thing for your ride. Wear the right pair of jeans, the right shirt, and the right pair of breakaway pants. There is no need for fancy clothing when you have a horse. Just make sure you look like a professional and that you are well equipped.
